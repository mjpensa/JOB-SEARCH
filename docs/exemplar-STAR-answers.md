## Notes on what "good" looks like for Matthew's STAR+R answers:
- Situation: 2 sentences max. Set the scene with company, problem, and stakes.
- Task: 1 sentence. What was specifically his responsibility.
- Action: 3-5 sentences. Concrete steps taken. Use "I" not "we" — specify his contribution.
- Result: 1-2 sentences. Quantified where possible. Business impact, not just task completion.
- Reflection (the +R): 1-2 sentences connecting the experience to the target role. This is where the AI governance bridge happens — "This experience directly translates to [specific AI governance challenge] because..."
- Total speaking time: 90 seconds to 2 minutes.
- Tone: Senior consultant who's done the work, not someone reciting bullet points.
